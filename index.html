SnapPixa Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

SnapPixa does not collect any personal data from users.

The App does not collect:

  • Personal information (such as name, email, location, identifiers)
  • Device information
  • Screenshot content
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports (other than those optionally collected by Apple)
  • Any data transmitted off your device

All processing happens locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

Since SnapPixa does not collect any information, no data is processed, analyzed, stored, or shared in any form.

3. Third-Party Services

SnapPixa does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. The App operates entirely on-device.

4. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data leakage or unauthorized access.

5. Children’s Privacy

SnapPixa does not target children and does not collect any data from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.
